Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

FPOP Company Limited ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the FPOP Inventory Management System. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

2. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service:

  • Email Address: Used for authentication and communication
  • Password: Securely stored for account access
  • User Profile Information: Name, role, and activity data
  • Usage Data: Login times, activities, sales records, and inventory transactions
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and operating system

3. Data Security

Your data security is important to us. We implement comprehensive security measures including:

  • Firebase authentication for secure user verification
  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS
  • Role-based access control (Admin vs. User)
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Use of Data

FPOP Company Limited uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service
  • To notify you about changes to our Service
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features of our Service
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather analysis or valuable information to improve our Service
  • To monitor inventory and sales activities
  • To detect and prevent illegal activities

5. Disclosure of Data

FPOP Company Limited will not share your personal information with third parties except when required by law or when you explicitly consent. Admin users have access to user sales data and activity logs as part of the inventory management system functionality.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request a data export

7. Retention of Data

Your personal data will be retained for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "effective date" at the top of this Privacy Policy.
